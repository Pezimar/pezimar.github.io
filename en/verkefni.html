Healthier Steps Through Europe

Healthier Steps Through Europe

Healthier Steps Through Europe is a three country project that i participated in between mars 2015 until january 2016. We had a group from Iceland (my group), from Sweden and from Canary Islands. The goal was to spread health and tolarence through Europe. I met amazing people made good friends and learnd a whole bunch.
